About Us

The story behind Pilani NGO and the hearts that power it.

Est.
2024

Born from Love for Pilani

A grassroots initiative by passionate locals who believe that small, consistent acts of kindness can transform a city.

Pilani NGO was founded in 2024 by a group of young volunteers from Pilani who noticed a growing need for compassionate action in their community — stray animals going hungry, birds struggling in the summer heat, trees disappearing from the landscape, and families shivering through winter without adequate clothing.

What started as a few friends feeding stray dogs on Loharu Road has grown into a structured initiative with four core programmes, a network of dedicated volunteers, and the unwavering support of Pilani's residents.

We believe that you don't need millions to make a difference. Sometimes, all it takes is a bowl of water for a bird, a sapling planted in barren soil, or a warm blanket handed to someone in need.

🤝

Transparency

Our Vision

"To build a Pilani where no animal goes hungry, no land remains barren, no bird is without water, and no person shivers without warmth."

We envision a future where the people of Pilani — and eventually all of Rajasthan — live in harmony with nature, treating every creature with dignity and every patch of earth as sacred. Our road map is simple: start small, stay consistent, and let compassion lead the way.
