What We Do

Four core initiatives that drive our mission to create a more compassionate Pilani.

🐾 Stray Animal Care

Providing daily food, clean water, and basic medical aid to stray dogs, cows, and other animals across Pilani.

Learn More →

🌳 Tree Plantation

Planting native trees across Pilani and surrounding villages to combat heat and restore green cover in Rajasthan.

Learn More →

🐦 Bird Nests & Water

Creating handmade nests and placing water stations for birds — especially during Rajasthan's harsh summers.

Learn More →

🧥 Clothing Distribution

Distributing warm clothes and blankets to the underprivileged during Pilani's cold winters.

Learn More →
