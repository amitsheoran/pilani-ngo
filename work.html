Our Work

Four missions, one goal — a compassionate Pilani.

🐾 Stray Animal Care

Hundreds of stray animals in Pilani depend on the kindness of humans for their survival. Our volunteers provide daily food, clean drinking water, and arrange basic veterinary care for injured or sick strays.

  • Daily feeding drives across Pilani — Loharu Road, Bus Stand, and New Colony
  • Emergency medical aid and vaccination camps for stray dogs
  • Cow shelters supported with fodder during monsoon and winter
  • Community awareness programmes to promote humane treatment of animals

🌳 Tree Plantation

Rajasthan's arid climate makes every tree precious. We organise regular plantation drives, planting native species like Neem, Peepal, and Babool that thrive in the local soil and provide shade, fruit, and cleaner air for decades to come.

  • 200+ trees planted across Pilani and nearby villages
  • Post-plantation care with regular watering schedules
  • School partnerships to educate children about environmental conservation
  • Focus on native, drought-resistant species suited to Rajasthan

🐦 Bird Nests & Water

Rajasthan's scorching summers are deadly for birds. We install handmade nests and place water bowls across rooftops, parks, temples, and public areas so that no bird goes thirsty.

  • 100+ handmade nests installed across Pilani
  • Water stations maintained daily during peak summer (April–July)
  • Grain and seed distribution at key locations
  • Awareness campaigns encouraging residents to place water on their rooftops

🧥 Clothing Distribution

Pilani's winters are harsh, with temperatures dropping below 5°C. Every year, we collect and distribute warm clothes, blankets, and shawls to those who need them most — daily wage workers, elderly residents, and homeless families.

  • Annual winter drive distributing 300+ garments and blankets
  • Collection points set up across Pilani for public donations
  • Targeted distribution in Ward No. 30 and surrounding slum areas
  • Children's clothing and school supplies included in drives
